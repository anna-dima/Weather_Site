The weather page is where you can input a location and it will show you 5 days of weather for that location. On the top right there is search bar, you will input the location that you would like for it to populate.


Beneath the search bar there is a clock with the current time as well as the date of today. There is also a box that shows the humidity,pressure and wind speed of today for your desired location.


At the bottom, there is a row that will show you the high and low temperature for the 5 days, including todays temperature. There is also an image that shows the precipitatom.
